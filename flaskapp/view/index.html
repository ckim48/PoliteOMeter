public About us

Polite-O-Meter utilizes Machine Learning in training the logistic model which could return a percentage of politeness level. Our dataset contains a total of 272 audios, including 16 neutral audios, 128 polite audios and 128 impolite audios. In order to deal with voice differences among different people, we ask for a neutral audio at first during microphone testing as reference. To help you improve politeness in different professional situations, we also provide different scenarios. According to logistic prediction, we have an accuracy of 78.12%. Hope you enjoy it!
